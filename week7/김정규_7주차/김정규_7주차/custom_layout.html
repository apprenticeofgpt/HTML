About the Yankees

The New York Yankees are a professional baseball team based in the Bronx, New York City. Founded in 1901, they became the Yankees in 1913 and have since become the most decorated team in Major League Baseball history with 27 World Series championships.

Legendary Players

  • Babe Ruth - "The Sultan of Swat"
  • Lou Gehrig - "The Iron Horse"
  • Joe DiMaggio - 56-game hitting streak
  • Mickey Mantle - Power and speed
  • Derek Jeter - "The Captain"
